# Brasília Congress — High-Performance Mesh (50M elements)

Ultra-refined mesh for HPC execution on supercomputers.

## Configuration

- **Base cell size**: 7.5 m (vs 15 m for standard case)
- **Target mesh**: ~50 M elements
- **Finest cells**: 0.8–1.0 m (Congress complex)
- **Solver**: simpleFoam, kEpsilon, steady-state RANS
- **Parallel**: Decomposed for 128–512 cores

## Case structure

```

        ├── fvSolution
        └── controlDict
```

## Quick start

```bash
cd Brasilia_HPC
./pipeline_hpc.sh all        # Full pipeline: geometry → mesh → decompose → solve
```

Single stages:
```bash
./pipeline_hpc.sh mesh       # blockMesh + snappyHexMesh (requires ~128 GB RAM)
./pipeline_hpc.sh decompose  # preparePar (domain decomposition for 128 cores)
./pipeline_hpc.sh solve      # simpleFoam on 128 cores
```

## HPC submission example (SLURM)

```bash
#!/bin/bash
#SBATCH --job-name=brasilia_cfd
#SBATCH --ntasks=128
#SBATCH --cpus-per-task=1
#SBATCH --time=48:00:00
#SBATCH --mem-per-cpu=2G

cd $SLURM_SUBMIT_DIR/openfoam
source /software/OpenFOAM/etc/bashrc

mpirun -np 128 simpleFoam -parallel | tee log.simpleFoam
```

## Mesh refinement (HPC)

| Stage | Base cell | SNH near | SNH far | Finest | Est. cells | Est. RAM (mesh) |
|-------|-----------|----------|---------|--------|------------|-----------------|
| Coarse (prod) | 20 m | 3 | 2 | 2.5 m | 7 M | 2 GB |
| Medium (prod) | 15 m | 3 | 2 | 1.9 m | 7 M | 2 GB |
| Fine (HPC) | 10 m | 3 | 2 | 1.25 m | 20 M | 8 GB |
| **Ultra (HPC)** | **7.5 m** | **4** | **3** | **0.94 m** | **50 M** | **20–30 GB** |

Each level halves the base cell size, multiplying the mesh by ~8–10× due to snappyHexMesh refinement in the Congress zone.

## Estimated performance

- **Meshing**: ~2–4 hours (requires 128 GB RAM for snappyHexMesh)
- **Solver (128 cores)**: ~36–48 hours to convergence (500 iterations)
- **Total runtime**: ~2–3 days on typical HPC cluster

## Geometry improvements

- **Congress platform**: Full 143 m × 216 m × 9 m slab (vs OSM box)
- **Twin towers**: Thin E-W slabs (50 m × 8 m × 100 m each)
- **Senado dome**: Hemisphere r=20 m, crown at 29 m
- **Câmara bowl**: Inverted hemisphere r=20 m, rim at 29 m

See `fix_congress_geometry.py` for implementation.

## Notes

- All geometry fixed via `fix_congress_geometry.py` (removes 2829 OSM Congress faces, adds 5 precise primitives)
- Finest cells (0.94 m) resolve Congress complex details
- Parallel decomposition uses simple slicing; metis available if needed
- Expect ~50 M cells to finish in ~40–50 hours on 128 cores (vs ~8.5 hours for 7 M on Pi 5 single-core)
